Our Performance

The work we put in every year is reflected by our performance at competition.

Lightweight

Our latest car, B15, was one of the lightest at competition, weighing in at 314lb.

5th in Design

Exceptional engineers on the team soared B15's design into one of the highest ranks at the Lincoln competition

6th in Skidpad

B15's lateral acceleration of 1.4g allowed us to dominate the skidpad event.

6th in Business Presentation

Our awesome business team pitched B15 to the judges, and won us 6th place with their presentation.
